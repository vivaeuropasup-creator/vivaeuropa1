How long does it take to get a residence permit?

Is it possible to speed up the process?

There are no official ways to accelerate, but we can minimize delays:

  • Proper document preparation from the first time
  • Quick response to authority requests
  • Professional process support

What happens after document submission?

The review process includes several stages:

  • 2-4 months: Main application review

We track status at each stage and inform you of progress.

Do I need to be in Spain during review?

Depends on the type of residence permit:

  • Golden Visa: Permanent presence not required
  • Work residence permit: Usually required to be in country
  • Non-lucrative residence permit: Presence recommended
  • Family reunification: Depends on specific situation

We'll explain requirements for your residence permit type during consultation.

üìÑ Documents

What documents are needed for residence permit?

Basic document list (specific requirements depend on residence permit type):

  • Passport (valid for at least 1 year)
  • Criminal record certificate from all countries of residence
  • Medical certificate from Spanish doctor
  • Documents confirming financial solvency
  • Documents confirming basis for residence permit
  • Translations of all documents into Spanish
  • Apostilles on all documents

We provide complete list after analyzing your situation.

Do I need apostille on all documents?

Yes, all documents from other countries must be apostilled:

  • Apostille is placed in the country of document issuance
  • After apostille, document is translated into Spanish
  • Translation must be certified by sworn translator
  • We help with apostilling and translations

How long are documents valid?

Validity periods vary by document types:

  • Criminal record certificate: 3 months from issue date
  • Medical certificate: 3 months
  • Education documents: no limitations
  • Birth/marriage certificates: no limitations

We monitor validity periods and remind about renewal necessity.

Can I submit incomplete documents?

Not recommended, but possible in some cases:

  • Incomplete package may lead to rejection
  • Authorities may request missing documents
  • We recommend submitting complete package immediately

In exceptional cases, we'll help with phased document submission.

üè† Living in Spain

Do I need to know Spanish?

Language requirements depend on residence permit type:

  • Golden Visa: language knowledge not required
  • Non-lucrative residence permit: basic level desirable
  • Work residence permit: depends on profession
  • Family reunification: basic level

We provide translator for important meetings and help with documents.

Where is it better to live in Spain?

City choice depends on your priorities:

  • Madrid: business center, many opportunities
  • Barcelona: cosmopolitan seaside city
  • Valencia: balance of city and beach, IT cluster

We'll help choose optimal city for your goals.

Cities overview

What taxes need to be paid?

Tax obligations depend on status:

  • Tax residents: income tax on worldwide income
  • Non-residents: tax only on Spanish income
  • Golden Visa: special benefits first years

We provide basic tax consultation as part of services.

Can I work with residence permit?

Work rights depend on residence permit type:

  • Work residence permit: full work rights
  • Golden Visa: work and business rights
  • Non-lucrative residence permit: cannot work
  • Family reunification: usually with work rights

We'll help choose residence permit type according to your plans.
